Marley A. Gregory

Marley is an actor, stunt man, and world traveler. With nearly a decade of theatrical and onscreen experience, he has been seen in "The Enhanced" Amazon Direct Series, in "The Eighth Voyage of Sindbad Stunt Show" at Universal Studios Orlando and on stages across North America and England. He aspires to continue his passion for acting and spreading meaningful messages through the craft until the day he dies.
